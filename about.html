The Vision Behind Shorveer

Hello! I am Jatin Sharma, operating from the Samchana region of Haryana. The creation of Shorveer Notes stems from a very personal journey and a clear mission. Preparing for crucial milestones like the Class 12 board exams and the NDA taught me the undeniable value of structured, high-quality, and accessible study material.

I built this platform to provide students with premium notes, quick revisions, and previous year questions all in one place—completely free of distractions. My goal is to empower every student to learn like a "Shorveer" (Warrior) and conquer their academic challenges.

My Journey & Aspirations

Beyond academics, I have a deep-rooted passion for the Indian armed forces and aviation, aspiring to serve as an officer in the Indian Air Force or work within defense technology. This discipline and motivation reflect directly in how I structure my content and run this platform.

Join the Community

Education is just one part of the journey. For daily motivation, life lessons, and high-energy edits, I also run a YouTube channel dedicated to keeping the fire alive.
