ABOUT OUR NOTES

How Shorveer Notes Are Crafted

We don't just copy-paste. Every single PDF and revision sheet on this platform is a result of deep research, analysis, and a student-first approach.

📖
The NCERT Core

The foundation of all our notes is the strictly updated CBSE NCERT curriculum. We extract the absolute essence of the textbook, ensuring that no crucial line or hidden concept is missed. If it's in the NCERT, it's simplified here.

📚
Premium Reference Books

To make complex topics easy to understand, we synthesize explanations from top-tier reference books (like HC Verma, SL Arora, Pradeep's, etc.). We take the best diagrams and the easiest definitions to save you hours of reading.

🎯
10-Year PYQ Analysis

Before writing a chapter's notes, we analyze the last 10 years of CBSE Board papers. Topics that are frequently asked are specially marked as "Most Important" or "High Yield" right inside the notes.

🧠
Topper's Methodology

The notes are structured for maximum brain retention. Using bullet points, flowcharts, mnemonics, and bold highlights, we ensure that during quick revisions, your eyes land exactly on the keywords the examiner wants to see.

Crafted by a Student, For the Students

Every piece of content on Shorveer Notes is handwritten, digitized, and cross-checked by Jatin Sharma. The goal is simple: You shouldn't have to spend 5 hours making notes when you can spend those 5 hours actually learning and practicing.
